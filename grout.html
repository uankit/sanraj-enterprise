SANGRACE Tile Grout

1 Kg

SANGRACE Tile grout is a polymer based cementitious tile joint filler which is used for filling the gap between the tiles.It effectively fills the gap and levels itself to give hard wearing , non dusting tile joints . It firmly holds the tiles together to give a long life to the wall & floor tiles. It is available in White and Ivory colour only.

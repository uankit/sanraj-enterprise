Plain Velcro

Width : 2 inch

Length : 25 meter

Plain Velcro tape roll is ideal for displaying tile onto cassette . Before using it remove dust from back of the tile and clean it with a wet cloth. Cut plain velcro tape roll (male) into desired pieces and paste it onto back of the tile with the help of dendrite/heatx/equivalent and leave it for one day to set it firmly.

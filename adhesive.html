SANRAJ Tile Adhesive

30 Kg

Sanraj wall and floor tile adhesive is TYPE 1polymer modified grey cement based powder which is specially designed for dry areas of interior wall & floor installation. It is ideal for small format like 300x300 MM ,600X600 MM , 800X800 MM In floor and 250x375 MM ,300X450 MM in wall .

SANRAJ + Tile Adhesive

20 Kg

Sanraj + wall and floor tile adhesive is TYPE 2 high polymer modified grey cement based powder which is specially designed for interior/exterior wall & floor installation. It is ideal for medium format like 600x1200 mm , 800 x1600 mm in floor and 300x600 mm , 400 x 800 mm , 600x600 mm in wall.

ELVON Tile Adhesive

Elvon super strength wall & floor is TYPE 3 multipurpose very high polymer enhanced with high flexibility grey cement based powder which is specially designed for interior/exterior wall & floor installation and tile on tile application. It is ideal for fixing big slabs , heavy duty tiles and stones in wall and floor.

Note : Before doing tile on tile application , ensure that the original tiling surface is in sound condition.
